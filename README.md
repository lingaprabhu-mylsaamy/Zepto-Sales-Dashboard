# Zepto- Sales & Delivey analytics Dashboard
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Zepto's sales, customer behavior, payment methods, delivery performance, and product category trends. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor business KPIs, identify operational bottlenecks, and make data-driven decisions.

The objective of this dashboard is to:
* Monitor overall business performance.
* Track revenue and customer growth.
* Analyze delivery efficiency.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Understand customer payment preferences.
* Measure customer satisfaction through ratings.

# Dashboard Insights
1) Revenue Performance💰 
Generated a total revenue of 36 Million.
Revenue remained relatively stable throughout the analysis period with a few seasonal fluctuations.
Peak revenue reached approximately 1.67M, while the lowest month recorded around 1.29M.
📦 Delivery Performance
50.32% of orders were delivered late.
49.68% of orders were delivered on time.

Insight:
Delivery performance is almost evenly split, indicating an opportunity to improve operational efficiency and reduce late deliveries.

2) Order Status Analysis🚚 
Approximately 85% of all orders were successfully delivered.
Around 10% of orders were cancelled.
Nearly 5% of orders were returned.

Insight:
A high delivery success rate indicates effective order fulfillment, though reducing cancellations and returns could further improve customer satisfaction.

3) Payment Mode Analysis💳 

UPI is the most preferred payment method, contributing the highest revenue.

Followed by:
* Card
* Cash on Delivery (COD)
* Net Banking
* Wallet Payments

Insight:
Digital payment methods dominate customer transactions, with UPI leading significantly.

4) Revenue by Product Category🛍 

Top revenue-generating categories include:
* Baby Care
* Personal Care
* Grocery & Staples
* Household
* Fruits & Vegetables

Lower revenue categories:
* Dairy & Bakery
* Beverages
* Snacks & Branded Foods

Insight:
Baby Care and Personal Care products contribute the highest share of revenue, making them key business segments.

5) Customer Ratings⭐ 

Average customer rating is 3.88/5.

Highest-rated category:
Processed Foods (~3.90)

Lowest-rated category:
Snacks & Beverages (~3.86)

Insight:
Customer ratings remain fairly consistent across categories, suggesting stable service quality with room for improvement.
